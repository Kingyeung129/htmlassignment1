Getting Around







With facilities such as underground car parks and an efficient internal transportation network of comfortable buses and beach shuttles, you will always have convenient connections between attractions and different parts of the island.

Travel for FREE within the island with the Sentosa Express, Sentosa Bus and Beach Shuttle!



Express Stations VivoCity Station Located on the 3rd level of VivoCity, the station will provide easy access to and from the HarbourFront MRT Station. Operating Hours First Train - 7am Last Train - 12mn Resorts World Station Located in front of Resorts World Sentosa, the Resorts World Station is a stop not to be missed! Operating Hours First Train - 7am Last Train - 12mn Imbiah Station Hop on down for convenient access to the Imbiah attractions cluster. Operating Hours First Train - 7am Last Train - 12mn Beach Station Alight here to Palawan and Siloso Beach, pubs, restaurants, food court and many other food and retail outlets.
